Formulation#

Grid#

\[\begin{split} \left[ {\begin{array}{cc} \mathbf Y_{vv} & \mathbf Y_{vi} \\ \mathbf Y_{iv} & \mathbf Y_{ii} \\ \end{array} } \right] \left[ {\begin{array}{c} \mathbf{\underline v}_k \\ \mathbf{\underline v}_u \\ \end{array} } \right] = \left[ {\begin{array}{c} \mathbf{\underline i}_u \\ \mathbf{\underline i}_k \\ \end{array} } \right] \end{split}\]
\[ \mathbf g_{cplx} = -\mathbf Y_{ii} \mathbf{\underline v}_u - \mathbf Y_{iv} \mathbf{\underline v}_k \]

Loads#

Phasor voltages and neutral voltage are defined as:

\[ \underline v_k = v_{lk}^r + jv_{lk}^i \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]

Phasor currents and neutral currents are defined as:

\[ \underline i_k = i_{lk}^r + ji_{lk}^i \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]

The vectors for the new algebraic equations \(\mathbf g_l\), the new algebraic states \(\mathbf y_l\) and the new inputs \(\mathbf u_l\) are defined as follows:

\[\begin{split} \mathbf g_l = \left[ \begin{array}{c} p_a + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_a - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_a^*\right) \\ p_b + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_b - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_b^*\right) \\ p_c + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_c - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_c^*\right) \\ q_a + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_a - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_a^*\right) \\ q_b + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_b - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_b^*\right) \\ q_c + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_c - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_c^*\right) \\ \Re \left(\underline i_a+ \underline i_b+ \underline i_c+ \underline i_n\right) \\ \Im \left(\underline i_a+ \underline i_b+ \underline i_c+ \underline i_n\right) \\ \end{array} \right] \;\;\;\;\;\;\; \mathbf y_l = \left[ \begin{array}{c} i_{la}^r \\ i_{lb}^r \\ i_{lc}^r \\ i_{la}^i \\ i_{lb}^i \\ i_{lc}^i \\ i_{ln}^r \\ i_{ln}^i \\ \end{array} \right] \;\;\;\;\;\;\; \mathbf u_l = \left[ \begin{array}{c} p_a \\ p_b \\ p_c \\ q_a \\ q_b \\ q_c \\ \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

VSC feeder#

AC side#

\[ \underline v_k = v_{lk}^r + jv_{lk}^i \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]

Phasor currents and neutral currents are defined as:

\[ \underline i_k = i_{lk}^r + ji_{lk}^i \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]

The vectors for the new algebraic equations \(\mathbf g_l\), the new algebraic states \(\mathbf y_l\) and the new inputs \(\mathbf u_l\) are defined as follows:

\[\begin{split} \mathbf g_{vsc} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} p_a + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_a - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_a^*\right) \\ p_b + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_b - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_b^*\right) \\ p_c + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_c - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_c^*\right) \\ q_a + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_a - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_a^*\right) \\ q_b + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_b - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_b^*\right) \\ q_c + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_c - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_c^*\right) \\ \Re \left(\underline i_a+ \underline i_b+ \underline i_c+ \underline i_n\right) \\ \Im \left(\underline i_a+ \underline i_b+ \underline i_c+ \underline i_n\right) \\ \end{array} \right] \;\;\;\;\;\;\; \mathbf y_{vsc} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} i_{la}^r \\ i_{lb}^r \\ i_{lc}^r \\ i_{la}^i \\ i_{lb}^i \\ i_{lc}^i \\ i_{ln}^r \\ i_{ln}^i \\ \end{array} \right] \;\;\;\;\;\;\; \mathbf u_{vsc} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} p_a \\ p_b \\ p_c \\ q_a \\ q_b \\ q_c \\ \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]

Losses#

\[ i_{k}^{rms} = \sqrt{ i_{kr}^2 + i_{ki}^2 } \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]
\[ p_{loss}^k = A + B i_{k}^{rms} + C \left( i_{k}^{rms} \right)^2 \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]
\[ p_{loss} = p_{loss}^a + p_{loss}^b +p_{loss}^c + p_{loss}^n\]

DC side#

\[ p_{dc} = p_{ac} - p_{loss} \]
\[ i_{dc} = \frac{p_{dc}}{v_{dc}} \]
\[ v_{dc} = v_{ar} - v_{nr} \]
\[ i_{ar} = i_{dc} \]
\[ i_{nr} = - i_{dc}\]

Sintax#

Inputs#

  • \(p_a\), \(p_b\), \(p_b\): p_vsc_a_<ac bus name>, p_vsc_b_<ac bus name>, p_vsc_c_<ac bus name>

  • \(q_a\), \(q_b\), \(q_b\): q_vsc_a_<ac bus name>, q_vsc_b_<ac bus name>, q_vsc_c_<ac bus name>

Some algebraic states#

  • \(p_{dc}\): p_vsc_<dc bus name>

  • \(p_{loss}\): p_vsc_loss_<ac bus name>

VSC DC former#

AC side#

\[ \underline v_k = v_{lk}^r + jv_{lk}^i \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]

Phasor currents and neutral currents are defined as:

\[ \underline i_k = i_{lk}^r + ji_{lk}^i \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c,\,n \]

The vectors for the new algebraic equations \(\mathbf g_l\), the new algebraic states \(\mathbf y_l\) and the new inputs \(\mathbf u_l\) are defined as follows:

\[\begin{split} \mathbf g_{vsc} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} p_a + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_a - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_a^*\right) \\ p_b + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_b - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_b^*\right) \\ p_c + \Re \left(\left(\underline v_c - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_c^*\right) \\ q_a + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_a - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_a^*\right) \\ q_b + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_b - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_b^*\right) \\ q_c + \Im \left(\left(\underline v_c - \underline v_n\right) \underline i_c^*\right) \\ \Re \left(\underline i_a+ \underline i_b+ \underline i_c+ \underline i_n\right) \\ \Im \left(\underline i_a+ \underline i_b+ \underline i_c+ \underline i_n\right) \\ \end{array} \right] \;\;\;\;\;\;\; \mathbf y_{vsc} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} i_{la}^r \\ i_{lb}^r \\ i_{lc}^r \\ i_{la}^i \\ i_{lb}^i \\ i_{lc}^i \\ i_{ln}^r \\ i_{ln}^i \\ \end{array} \right] \;\;\;\;\;\;\; \mathbf u_{vsc} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} p_a \\ p_b \\ p_c \\ q_a \\ q_b \\ q_c \\ \end{array} \right] \end{split}\]
\[ p_k = \Lambda_k \left( p_{dc} + p_{loss} \right) \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; k = a,\,b,\,c \]

Alothugh \(\Lambda_a\), \(\Lambda_b\) and \(\Lambda_c\) can be arbitrary, special care must be consider in order to satisfy:

\[ \Lambda_a + \Lambda_b + \Lambda_c = 1 \]
